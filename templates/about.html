Understanding Heart Disease: Causes, Risks, and Prevention

📌 Causes of Heart Disease

Heart disease can be triggered by several factors, including:
  • 🍔 Unhealthy Diet
  • 🚬 Smoking & Excessive Alcohol
  • 🏃‍♂️Lack of Physical Activity
  • 🧬 Genetics & Family History – Higher risk if heart disease runs in the family
  • 🍩 Diabetes & Metabolic Disorders – Affects blood sugar and damages arteries

⚠️ Risk Factors for Heart Disease

Several factors increase your chances of developing heart disease:
  • ⏳ Aging
  • 🍟 Poor Eating Habits
  • 🏡 Family History
  • 😓 Chronic Stress & Mental Health Issues
  • 💓 High Blood Pressure (Hypertension)

🛡️ Prevention Tips: Protect Your Heart!

You can reduce your heart disease risk by following these simple steps:
  • 🥗 Eat a Balanced Diet
  • 🏋️‍♂️ Stay Active
  • 🚭 Avoid Smoking & Limit Alcohol
  • 🧘 Manage Stress
  • 🩺 Regular Check-ups
